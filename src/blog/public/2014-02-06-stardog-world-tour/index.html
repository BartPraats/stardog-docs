Stardog World Tour

By Mike Grove

We’re giving talks about Stardog at meetups on the East Coast over the next few months:

  • Washington DC, February 18th at 6:30pm
  • Baltimore MD, February 27th at 6:30pm
  • Philadephia PA, March 4th at 6:30pm
  • New York, NY, May 22nd at 6:30pm

We’ll also be announcing dates in Northern Virginia, Boston, and Toronto in the coming weeks. It’s a great opportunity to meet some of the folks behind Stardog and learn more about it.
